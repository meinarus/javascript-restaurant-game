Our Story

The History of Sizzlog House

📖

Sizzlog House started from late-night study cravings when we realized our favorite takeout silog meals always got cold too fast. We decided to combine the classic tapsilogan menu with heavy cast-iron sizzling plates so that every single bite stays hot, fresh, and full of flavor — from the first scoop of garlic rice to the last.

🍽️ Our Sizzling Menu

What's Cooking Today?

Every dish is served on a roaring hot cast-iron plate. Pick your favorite silog!

1. Sizzling Tapsilog

Thinly sliced, tender beef tapa marinated in our secret sweet and savory sauce. Served roaring hot so the edges get perfectly caramelized on the cast-iron plate.

2. Sizzling Tocilog

Juicy pork tocino with the perfect balance of sweet and garlicky flavors. The sizzling plate glazes the pork in its own natural syrups for a mouth-watering sticky finish.

3. Sizzling Pork Sisig

Crunchy, savory, and slightly spicy chopped pork face that crackles with every bite. Topped with fresh onions and a raw egg that cooks beautifully as you mix it.

4. Sizzling Bangsilog

A large, golden-fried milkfish belly marinated in vinegar and garlic. The hot plate keeps the skin perfectly crispy while the inside stays soft and flaky.

5. Sizzling Lechon Kawali

Deep-fried pork belly chunks served with savory lechon sauce. The sizzling iron pan ensures the fat melts perfectly while the skin stays crunchy.

6. Sizzling Burger Steak

Two thick and juicy beef patties smothered in a rich mushroom gravy. The gravy bubbles and thickens on the hot plate, perfect with our toasted garlic rice.

7. Sizzling Corned Beef

Premium, chunky corned beef sautéed with white onions and crispy potato cubes. The sizzling plate adds a smoky, toasted flavor to this classic breakfast favorite.

8. Sizzling Hotsilog

Two classic, juicy red hotdogs scored and grilled to perfection. A nostalgic childhood favorite given a fun, smoky upgrade on our hot iron plates.

9. Sizzling Chicken Inasal

Boneless chicken thigh marinated in lemongrass and vinegar, grilled until perfectly charred. We pour savory chicken oil over it right as it hits the sizzling plate.

10. Extra Sizzling Garlic Rice

Because one cup is never enough! Our garlic rice is extra toasted, extra garlicky, and kept piping hot on a mini cast-iron skillet.
